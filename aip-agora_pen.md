## Summary
<!--"If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough." Provide a simplified and layman-accessible explanation of the AIP.-->

This AIP is to describe a virtual volunteer newsroom - Agora PEN (or PEN) - a fundamental feature for the Agora community to streamline information flow. It also introduces a new governance framework - Republic of Agora - a foundational consensus mechanism for the community.

## Abstract
<!--A short (~200 word) description of the technical issue being addressed.-->

The Agora PEN (newsroom) is a fundamental feature for the Agora community to connect and share information to resist message manipulation and knowledge monopolies. It manifests the Agora's creed and ideals.
The team of PEN is composed of the Agora community volunteers. They collaborate remotely to create roadmaps, develop strategies, manage projects, and run a virtual media hub with the community and the world.
The governance of PEN is defined as the Republic of Agora (RoA), a consensus of Proof of Contribution (PoC). The introduction of RoA is to incentivize a renaissance of PoC for the Agora community.
